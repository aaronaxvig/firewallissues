## GPC-15977

Fixed an issue where, when the GlobalProtect app was installed on Android devices and configured with **Always-on (User logon)** mode and certificate authentication, the app failed to connect when certificate was renewed on the Microsoft Intune MDM.

## GPC-15231

Fixed an issue where, when the GlobalProtect app was installed on Android devices and connected with **Always-on (User logon)** option, the app displayed the following notifications twice and the users were unable to clear the notifications.

**Always on VPN is currently enabled. Disabling the VPN connection could result in loss of network access**.

## GPC-15023

Fixed an issue where, when the GlobalProtect app was installed on devices running on Chrome OS, the app could not connect to the gateway after successful SAML authentication. The app stayed in Connecting state and the users had to go back to the Portal Authentication Complete page and click on **click here to launch GlobalProtect** to connect to the gateway.
