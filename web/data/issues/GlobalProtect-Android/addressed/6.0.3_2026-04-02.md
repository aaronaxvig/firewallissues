## GPC-15088

Fixed an issue where, when the GlobalProtect app was installed on Android devices, the GlobalProtect notification displayed on the screen was unresponsive.

However, the notification continues to stay on the screen due to Android OS limitation. This issue is listed under the [Known Issues](/content/techdocs/en_US/globalprotect/release-notes/6-0/known-issues-related-to-gp-app.html#id1787F0E08SZ) section.
