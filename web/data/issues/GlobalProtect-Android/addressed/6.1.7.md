## GPC-21067

Fixed an issue where, when the GlobalProtect app was installed on Android devices, the app could not connect to the internal gateway when the SAML authentication method was used to authenticate to the app.

## GPC-22254

Fixed an issue where the GlobalProtect app got stuck in the Connecting stage when Okta SAML authentication was used to authenticate to the app.

## GPC-22586

Fixed an issue where, when the GlobalProtect app version 6.1.6 was installed on Android devices, the app stopped working because the ForegroundService did not function as expected.
