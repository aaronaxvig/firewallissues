## GPC-15068

A fix was made to address an OpenSSL infinite loop vulnerability in GlobalProtect app software ([CVE-2022-0778](https://security.paloaltonetworks.com/CVE-2022-0778)).

## GPC-15023

Fixed an issue where, when the GlobalProtect app was installed on devices running on Chrome OS, the app could not connect to the gateway after successful SAML authentication. The app stayed in **Connecting** state and the users had to go back to the **Portal Authentication Complete** page and click on **click here to launch GlobalProtect** to connect to the gateway.
